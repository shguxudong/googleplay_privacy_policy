1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the Relationship Linewise mobile application for Android and iOS and to privacy-related information presented through relationshiplinewise.com.

In this policy, "Relationship Linewise," "we," "us," and "our" refer to the developer and publisher of the Relationship Linewise application.

2. Information handled by Relationship Linewise

Relationship Linewise allows you to create and manage information such as the following:

Type of information Purpose Where it is stored
Scene maps with actors and relation lines To visualise who is involved in a situation and how they are connected. Locally on your device.
Boundary problem details (events, breakdown cards, action plans) To help you analyse a specific issue, rehearse a conversation, and plan next steps. Locally on your device.
Rehearsal drafts and conversation scripts To let you practise what you might say before having the real conversation. Locally on your device.
Saved scenes and scene history To keep your previous work available for later review. Locally on your device.
App preferences (language, avatar choices, privacy mode) To remember your personal settings and customisations. Locally on your device.
Custom avatars and avatar preferences To display personalised portraits for people in your scenes. Locally on your device.

This information is used only to provide the app's local features. Relationship Linewise does not operate a server that receives or stores this content.

3. Information we do not collect

In the current version of Relationship Linewise, we do not collect or transmit:

  • Your scene maps, boundary problems, rehearsal drafts, or action plans.
  • Your name, email address, phone number, or account credentials.
  • Your contacts, call history, SMS messages, or messaging-app content.
  • Your precise or approximate location.
  • Your photos, microphone recordings, camera data, or media library.
  • Your social-media accounts, profiles, stories, last-seen status, or activity.
  • Advertising identifiers for targeted advertising.
  • Health, medical, diagnostic, or therapy records.

Relationship Linewise does not use advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, AI processing services, social-login services, or cloud-sync services.

4. Local storage and retention

Information created in Relationship Linewise is stored locally on your device. We cannot view, retrieve, recover, or remotely delete this information because it is not uploaded to us.

Local information remains on your device until you delete it within the app, clear the app's storage through your device settings, or uninstall the app.

Device backups may include application data depending on your operating-system and backup settings. Those backups are controlled by you and the relevant platform provider.

5. Backup and restore

Relationship Linewise includes a built-in backup and restore function so you can save and recover your local data between installations or device changes.

Backup files contain only your own locally stored app content such as saved scenes, boundary problem details, and preferences. The backup file is managed entirely by you.

When you restore a backup, the backup file replaces your current local data with the content it contains. Be careful not to merge incompatible backup versions.

If you share a backup file with someone else, they will be able to read the private scenes and boundary problem details it contains. Treat backup files as confidential.

6. Privacy mode

Relationship Linewise offers a privacy mode that hides the titles and previews of your saved scenes from the home screen. This helps keep your relationship-related work discreet when others might see your device.

Enabling privacy mode only obscures the displayed text. The actual scene content remains fully accessible inside the app when you choose to view it.

7. Multiple saved scenes

You can save multiple independent scenes for later review. Each saved scene preserves its title, actors, relations, boundary problems, and action plans exactly as you left them.

Saved scenes can be renamed, duplicated, or deleted at any time from within the app. Deleting a scene removes only that specific scene from your local storage.

8. Multilingual support

Relationship Linewise is available in several languages including English, Arabic, German, Spanish, French, Japanese, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese.

Your language preference is stored locally on your device and is used only to display interface text in your preferred language.

9. Deleting your data

You can delete individual saved scenes from within the app.

You can also use the app's "Delete all data" function (available in Settings) to remove all locally stored Relationship Linewise content and preferences.

You may additionally delete local app data through your device settings or by uninstalling Relationship Linewise.

Because we do not maintain user accounts or a remote database of app content, there is no server-side account or content deletion request to submit to us.

11. Security

Relationship Linewise is designed to minimize privacy risk by keeping sensitive app content on your device and by avoiding unnecessary accounts, servers, advertising, tracking, and external content-processing services.

The protection of locally stored information also depends on your:

  • Device security.
  • Operating-system security.
  • Screen lock and passcode.
  • Device backup settings.
  • Handling of exported or copied information.

No storage method or device can be guaranteed to be completely secure, especially if a device is lost, shared, compromised, or unlocked by another person.

We recommend using a device passcode and enabling privacy mode in Relationship Linewise when appropriate.

12. Children's privacy

Relationship Linewise is intended for adults and is not designed or directed toward children under 18.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided information to us outside the app, please contact us using the details below.

13. Safety and scope

Relationship Linewise is a relationship communication and boundary exercise tool. It is not:

  • Therapy.
  • Medical advice.
  • Legal advice.
  • Crisis support.
  • A diagnostic service.
  • A relationship counselling service.

Relationship Linewise does not predict another person's feelings or behaviour and does not make decisions for you.

If you are in immediate danger, may harm yourself or another person, or are experiencing abuse, coercion, stalking, or threats, contact local emergency services or an appropriate qualified professional support service.
