1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the PauseKits mobile application for Android and iOS and to privacy-related information presented through pausekits.com .

In this policy, “PauseKits,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to the developer and publisher of the PauseKits application.

2. Information handled by PauseKits

PauseKits allows you to create and manage information such as the following:

Type of information Purpose Where it is stored
Message drafts and unsent notes To help you write, pause, review, lock, or archive a draft before deciding what to do next. Locally on your device.
Pause, reset, and no-check sessions To operate timers, display the selected flow, and maintain local progress records. Locally on your device.
Trigger-day plans and reminder times To save plans and schedule optional local notifications. Locally on your device and within the device notification system.
Logistics message templates To let you edit, save, or copy practical communication drafts. Locally on your device.
App settings To remember preferences such as language, notification choices, onboarding status, and app-lock settings. Locally on your device.

This information is used only to provide the app's local features. PauseKits does not operate a server that receives or stores this content.

3. Information we do not collect

In the current version of PauseKits, we do not collect or transmit:

  • Your message drafts, unsent notes, trigger plans, or logistics scripts.
  • Your name, email address, phone number, or account credentials.
  • Your contacts, call history, SMS messages, or messaging-app content.
  • Your precise or approximate location.
  • Your photos, microphone recordings, camera data, or media library.
  • Your social-media accounts, profiles, stories, last-seen status, or activity.
  • Advertising identifiers for targeted advertising.
  • Health, medical, diagnostic, or therapy records.

PauseKits does not use advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, AI processing services, social-login services, or cloud-sync services.

4. Local storage and retention

Information created in PauseKits is stored locally on your device. We cannot view, retrieve, recover, or remotely delete this information because it is not uploaded to us.

Local information remains on your device until you delete it within the app, clear the app's storage through your device settings, or uninstall the app.

Device backups may include application data depending on your operating-system and backup settings. Those backups are controlled by you and the relevant platform provider.

5. Local notifications

PauseKits may ask for permission to schedule notifications for features such as:

  • A draft-unlock time.
  • A reset timer.
  • A no-check pause.
  • A trigger-day reminder.

These notifications are scheduled locally through your device's operating system. PauseKits does not use a remote push-notification server.

You may deny notification permission or disable notifications at any time in your device settings. Core app features remain available without notification permission.

6. App lock (PIN)

If you enable app lock, PauseKits will require a numeric PIN code each time the app is opened. Authentication is performed locally within the app — no PIN information is sent to external servers.

PauseKits does not collect, store, or transmit your PIN code. The PIN is used only for local access control on your device.

We recommend using a PIN code that is not easily guessable and that differs from common patterns (such as your birth date).

7. Data portability

Currently, PauseKits stores all content locally on your device. There is no built-in export function at this time.

Future versions may allow you to copy text, export local data, or share exported files through another app or storage location. These actions would occur only when you choose to initiate them.

Once information is copied, exported, or shared outside PauseKits, it is no longer controlled by the app. The privacy and security practices of the destination app, service, device, or person will apply.

8. Deleting your data

You can delete individual drafts, plans, sessions, or other records from within the app where the relevant option is available.

You can also use the app's “Delete all local data” function to remove locally stored PauseKits content and settings.

You may additionally delete local app data through your device settings or by uninstalling PauseKits.

Because we do not maintain user accounts or a remote database of app content, there is no server-side account or content deletion request to submit to us.

Exported copies and device backups must be deleted separately from the locations where you placed them.

9. Apple App Store and Google Play services

Apple and Google independently process information related to:

  • App discovery and store browsing.
  • App purchase and payment processing.
  • Installation and updates.
  • Store accounts and device compatibility.
  • Fraud prevention and refunds.
  • Platform diagnostics and store performance.

PauseKits does not receive your full payment-card details.

We may receive sales, transaction, installation, or diagnostic information that Apple or Google makes available to developers through their developer dashboards.

This platform information does not include the private drafts, notes, plans, or scripts you create inside PauseKits.

The platform providers' own privacy policies apply to their processing:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

10. Security

PauseKits is designed to minimize privacy risk by keeping sensitive app content on your device and by avoiding unnecessary accounts, servers, advertising, tracking, and external content-processing services.

The protection of locally stored information also depends on your:

  • Device security.
  • Operating-system security.
  • Screen lock and passcode.
  • Biometric settings.
  • Device backup settings.
  • Handling of exported or copied information.

No storage method or device can be guaranteed to be completely secure, especially if a device is lost, shared, compromised, or unlocked by another person.

We recommend using a device passcode and enabling the PauseKits app lock when available.

11. Children's privacy

PauseKits is intended for adults and is not designed or directed toward children under 18.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided information to us outside the app, please contact us using the details below.

12. Safety and scope

PauseKits is a self-reflection and communication-boundary utility. It is not:

  • Therapy.
  • Medical advice.
  • Legal advice.
  • Crisis support.
  • A diagnostic service.
  • A relationship-repair service.

PauseKits does not predict another person's feelings or behavior and does not make decisions for you.

If you are in immediate danger, may harm yourself or another person, or are experiencing abuse, coercion, stalking, or threats, contact local emergency services or an appropriate qualified professional support service.

13. Changes to this Privacy Policy

The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

If a future version introduces accounts, cloud sync, advertising, analytics, AI processing, remote crash reporting, or any other transfer of user data, this Privacy Policy and the relevant Apple App Store and Google Play privacy disclosures will be updated to reflect those changes.
